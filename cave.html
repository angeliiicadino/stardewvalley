Fruit Bats

Fruit bats leave various fruit in the Cave on the farm overnight. This is a great option for making Artisan Goods as it gives a slow but steady supply of fruit, particularly foraged berries, even out of their respective seasons.

Cherry

It's popular, and ripens sooner than most other fruits.

Pomegranate

Within the fruit are clusters of juicy seeds.

Peach

It's almost fuzzy to the touch.

Apple

A crisp fruit used for juice and cider.

Orange

Juicy, tangy, and bursting with sweet summer aroma.

Spice Berry

It fills the air with a pungent aroma.

Apricot

A tender little fruit with a rock-hard pit.

Wild Plum

Tart and juicy with a pungent aroma.

Blackberry

An early-fall treat.

Salmonberry

A spring-time berry with the flavor of the forest.

Mushrooms

Cave mushroom boxes produce every day. Six boxes will be added to the cave where certain types of mushrooms will randomly grow. Five different types will appear, including all four that are required to craft the Life Elixir.

Common Mushroom

Slightly nutty, with good texture.

Red Mushroom

A spotted mushroom sometimes found in caves.

Purple Mushroom

A rare mushroom found deep in caves.

Chanterelle

A tasty mushroom with a fruity smell and slightly peppery flavor.

Morel

Sought after for its unique nutty flavor.
