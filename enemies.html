All Floors


Green Slime

Slimes are enemies that can be found in various locations, including The Mines and the Secret Woods. They come in five different variations, all based on the same sprite and code (a color hue is added to the grey sprite found in data files). You need to kill 1000 Slimes of any color for Monster Eradication Goals. All Slimes in the game count towards this goal (e.g., in the Secret Woods) except for Big Slimes and Slimes raised in a Slime Hutch (by means of a Slime Incubator or Mating). Slimes spawned by killing a Big Slime do count towards the goal.

Floors 1 to 29


Bug

They fly up and down or left and right in a fixed path. They will ignore the Player and will only follow the same path until slain.

Cave Fly

They fly at you (including through walls) at high speeds, damaging you on contact. Then they loop around for another pass.

Duggy

Duggies can be found within dirt patches in the cave floor, attempting to pop up and attack the player. Duggies can move and sense the player for up to two tiles.

Grub

Grubs appear in groups and attack on sight. Upon sustaining critical damage, they will flee and attempt to pupate. When pupating, a Grub turns blue-green and is immune to all damage. It will emerge from its blue-green cocoon as a Cave Fly.

Rock Crab

They disguise themselves as stones, sometimes surprising the player. When immobile, they are invulnerable to attack, though they can be hit with a pickaxe to knock them back, making them mobile again.

Floors 31 to 39


Bats

Bats are enemies that can be found at different levels inside The Mines and Skull Cavern.

Stone Golem

Mostly hidden by the walls with their dark colors, they wander aimlessly, but damage the player if they collide. They have glowing eyes.

Floors 41 to 79


Dust Sprite

Bats are enemies that can be found at different levels inside The Mines and Skull Cavern.

Frost Bat

Bats are enemies that can be found at different levels inside The Mines and Skull Cavern.

Frost Jelly

Each attack slightly increases the chance to start glowing and increase its speed by 2.

Ghost

Ghosts will immediately hover towards the player upon entering the level. They will ignore physical obstacles, such as rocks, and can fly over the black space in the mines which the player cannot access.

Skeleton

Skeletons will approach and attack the player by collision. They also have a longer-ranged bone throw attack, which passes through rocks. This ranged attack is interrupted if the skeleton takes damage.

Floors 81 to 119


Lava Bat

The Mines, level 81-119, Skull Cavern levels 1-50.

Lava Crab

They disguise themselves as stones, sometimes surprising the player. When immobile, they are invulnerable to attack, though they can be hit with a pickaxe to knock them back, making them mobile again.

Metal Head

They have no special attacks.

Red Sludge

Can cause 16 damage with 0 defense at 2 speed.

Shadow Brute

They have no special attacks, but resist knockback.


Shadow Shaman

Shadow Shaman are magic users, with a healing spell that heals the Shadow Shaman itself and all enemies in the immediate area by 60 health points.

Squid Kid

They will move suddenly as well as hover over rocks, and shoot fireballs at the player, which ricochet off walls.

Skull Cavern


Armored Crab

They fly back and forth in a vertical straight line between walls or objects. They are not aggressive, but will deal damage to the player if touched from the front.

Big Slime

Big Slimes have similar attack behavior to the smaller slimes. When killed they have a 75% chance to split into 2-4 smaller slimes which will attack the player.

Carbon Ghost

They fly towards the player and will teleport to a random location after they hit the player. They can pass through walls and "black space" to reach the player.

Iridium Bat

Skull Cavern level 51+, and on Prehistoric Floors.

Iridium Crab

They disguise themselves as Iridium Nodes, sometimes surprising the player. When immobile, they are invulnerable to attack, though they can be hit with a pickaxe to knock them back, making them mobile again.

Lava Bat

The Mines, level 81-119, Skull Cavern levels 1-50.

Mummy

After being knocked down, they will get back up after 10 seconds unless killed by a bomb while downed. They will tremble slightly right before getting back up.

Pepper Rex

They slowly move around the cave floor and belch flame blasts at the player when they get too close.

Purple Slime

These are the most powerful slimes, found only in the Skull Cavern.

Serpent

Serpents will fly at the player at high velocities, dealing a fairly large amount of damage on hit. They are common, meaning that they can easily swarm the player if they do not have a strong enough weapon.

Quarry Mine


Copper Slime

Random dungeon Floors in The Mines.

Haunted Skull

Their behavior is similar to that of Bats and Serpents; they may fly away from the player and change the direction of attack.

Iron Slime

Random dungeon Floors in The Mines.

Mutant Bug Lair


Mutant Fly

They will fly towards the player (including through walls) at high speeds and deal damage on contact. They will then loop around for another pass.

Mutant Grub

Mutant Grubs appear in groups and attack on sight. Upon sustaining critical damage, they will flee and attempt to pupate.

Volcano Dungeon


Dwarvish Sentry

They move extremely slowly and are immune to knockback.


False Magma Cap

They disguise themselves as stationary Magma Caps, sometimes surprising the player. Their behavior is similar to Rock Crabs and Lava Crabs.

Hot Head

Like their Mine's counterparts Metal Head, they have a very high defense.

Lava Lurk

The Lava Lurk swims in lava, then pops its head out and shoots 4 fireballs that deal damage. A fireball can be destroyed by hitting it with a weapon.

Magma Sprite

They are very common, meaning that they can easily swarm the player with their stronger counterparts Magma Sparkers if they are not equipped with a strong enough weapon.

Magma Sparker

Like Slimes, it can "charge" the player, making a quick dash. They are common, meaning that they can easily swarm the player with their counterparts Magma Sprites if they are not equipped with a strong enough weapon.

Magma Duggy

They can be found within dirt patches on the cave floor, attempting to pop up and attack the player. Magma Duggies can move and sense the player up to two tiles away.

Tiger Slime

The Tiger Slime egg is a tiger-stripped blob with the description “can be hatched in a slime incubator.”
